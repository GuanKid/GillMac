About Us

Established in 2011 in Port Elizabeth (now Gqeberha), Eastern Cape, Gillmac Industrial & Commercial Systems has built a strong reputation in the industrial and commercial sector. The company started by collaborating on public sector projects, primarily within municipalities, where it developed a solid foundation of expertise and reliability.

Today, Gillmac operates as an independent contractor, delivering professional, high-quality solutions to clients while continuing to expand its capabilities and service offering
